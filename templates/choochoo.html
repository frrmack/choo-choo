There are many ways to use Choo Choo.

Cherry Pick

Explore a topic closer, and pick a few lectures or challenges in it to sharpen your skills. Then jump to a completely different topic. Choo Choo is your oyster. Complete freedom. Absolute anarchy.

Marathon

Learn everything Choo Choo has to offer by following a ten day program. Best if you want the learn it all, with a natural flow from one activity to another. Not for people with commitment issues.

Projects

Tackle a project that you can complete in a few days. Best way to learn is by figuring out answers to real problems. Completing a project will give you data science street cred and survival skills.
